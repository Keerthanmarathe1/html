States of India

Paragraph 1: Karnataka is Situated in the South west region of India. The capital city of the state is Bengaluru and the Official Language is Kannada.

Paragraph 2: Goa is Situated in the South western coast of India.The capital city of the state is Panaji and the Official Language is Konkani.

Paragraph 3: Maharashtra is Situated in the western penensular region of India.The capital city of the state is Mumbai and the Official Language is Marathi.

Paragraph 4: Gujarat is Situated in the western coast of India.The capital city of the state is Gandhinagar and the Official Language is Gujarati.

Paragraph 5: Kerala is Situated in the Malabar coast of India.The capital city of the state is Thiruvanantapuram and the Official Language is Malayalam.

Paragraph 6: Tamil Nadu is Situated in the Southern part of India.The capital city of the state is Chennai and the Official Language is Tamil.
